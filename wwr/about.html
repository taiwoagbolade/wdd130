White Water Rafting Co.

Our mission is to bring thrilling, safe, and unforgettable white water rafting adventures to nature lovers all levels. Join us and discover the exhilaration of the river and enjoy the beauty of nature!

History

White Water Rafting Co. launched its full operations on March 22, 2020, in the vibrant city of Lagos. Since that eciting day, we've proudly provided safe, adrenaline pumping adventures to hundreds of thrill-seekers, people of all ages, backgrounds and walks of life. From first-time rafters to seasoned paddlers, our mission has always been clear: create unforgettable experience that blend fun, nature, and the spirit of exploration, all while maintaining the highest safety standards. And we're just getting started.
